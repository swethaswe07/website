Mother Theresa Institute Of Engineering & Technology


The Mother Theresa Institute of Engineering and Technology (MTIET) is located at Melumoi Village and Post, Palamaner, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh 517408. It's on the Chennai Bombay National Highway, 5 kilometers from Palamaner.

About:

Mother Theresa Institute of Engineering and Technology, established in 2010, empowers rural youth with accessible engineering education. The Institute is promoted by the non-profit 'Holy Cross Educational Society, Palamaner.
